The Problem
Why WINDI Exists

Today, important decisions are made in systems that can be altered, deleted, or manipulated. Reports get rewritten. Versions are lost. Responsibilities dissolve.

WINDI was created to ensure that important human decisions are registered, protected, and auditable forever.

In WINDI, a "decision" is any formal statement with legal, operational, financial, or historical consequences — contracts, approvals, compliance reports, government notices, or audit findings.

Glossary
WINDI in Human Language
Forensic Ledger
Immutable proof register — records every decision with a cryptographic hash so it cannot be altered after the fact.
Virtue Receipt
Official proof that a decision followed all governance rules — the "seal of quality" for every document decision.
SGE Score
Risk and integrity score of a document (R0-R5). The higher the score, the more compliant the document. The SGE engine analyzes 6 semantic layers.
Governance Engine
The core system that checks risks, verifies compliance, and ensures every decision meets institutional rules.
ISP — Institutional Style Profile
Custom governance profile for each organization — defines templates, branding, compliance rules, and document standards.
Zero-Knowledge Architecture
WINDI never stores your sensitive data — only the cryptographic proof that a decision was correctly made. Data stays with the client.
