Schritt fur Schritt Step-by-Step Passo a Passo

1. Login with institutional credentials

2. Click "+ Neues Dokument" to create document

3. Use toolbar to format content

4. Ask WINDI Chat for assistance (optional)

5. Review and edit AI suggestions

6. Assign Prufer (Reviewer) if required

7. Click "Speichern" to save draft

8. Click "Abschliesen" to finalize

9. Export as PDF with WINDI-RECEIPT
